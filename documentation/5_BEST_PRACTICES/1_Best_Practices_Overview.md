The more specific you are, the more "human" and aligned with your voice the output will be. Vague prompts lead to generic results.

*   **Define Your Style**: Don't just ask to "write a scene." specify the tone. Is it *noir*, *whimsical*, *gritty*, or *cinematic*?
*   **Dictate the Pacing**: Tell SammyAI if a scene should be *fast-paced and punchy* or *slow and contemplative*.
*   **Provide Concrete Details**: Mention specific sensory details (smells, sounds, textures) you want included. This prevents the AI from falling into clichÃ© descriptions.
