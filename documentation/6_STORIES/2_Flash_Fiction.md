# Flash Fiction Archive

Flash fiction is the art of brevity - stories that deliver maximum impact in a minimal word count. This section showcases SammyAI's ability to craft concise, compelling narratives across various genres, from historical drama to surrealist explorations.

Each story below was developed starting from a simple prompt or inspiration, with SammyAI handling the vast majority of the creative heavy lifting.

---

## One Bad Year

> [!NOTE]
> **Story Summary**
> In 1934, Jebediah Thorne is a man consumed by the devastation of the Dust Bowl, his farm lost and his spirit broken. Then, on New Year's Eve, he experiences a disorienting temporal shift, finding himself inexplicably returned to that same year - 1934 - with a chilling awareness of the events about to unfold.
