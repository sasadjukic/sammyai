# 📺 TV Pilots Archive

This section highlights SammyAI's capacity to help you create TV pilots across different genres. As is the case with other works, the TV pilots displayed here involve an iterative process, utilizing multiple AI models to show that SammyAI can seamlessly continue your story started by a different model.

Prior to drafting these stories, short treatments and scene-by-scene breakdowns (two separate files) were submitted to SammyAI (mostly through RAG to avoid uploading the files again and again) to ensure that SammyAI was provided with better understanding of project's narrative and characters.

It is also important to note that not EVERY SINGLE word in these pilots had been written by SammyAI.
